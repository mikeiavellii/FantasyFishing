Welcome to the All Blue.


Here any and all fish can be caught.
Adjust your depth using the meter on the left and click the cast button when you're ready to fish.
Sometimes the fish just aren't biting, but if you do catch something, you can either keep it and add it to your profile, sell it, or release it.
Your caught list can be viewed over on your user profile. It will also display the trophies you've earned and a few other things so keep fishing in the All Blue and don't forget to check your profile often to see if you've earned anything new.

Happy Fishing!
